          • Annual Business Survey (ABS)

            From Office for National Statistics

            Data covering the production, construction, distribution, and service industries. Provides high-level indicators of economic activity from January 2008 to December 2019, for local authority down to postcode geography.

          • Annual Survey of Hours and Earnings (ASHE)

            From Office for National Statistics

            De-identified data on hours worked and earnings for UK employees by sex, full-time and part-time working, and postcode level geography, 1990 to 2020.

          • BBC News Sentiment Analysis

            From Office for National Statistics and BBC

            De-identified BBC article data for quantitative analysis of sentiment, web scraped publicly from news articles from 1 January 2019 to 31 October 2021. Contains over 51,000 records to enable analysis of BBC news coverage and sentiment by subnational areas and categories.

          • Birth estimates

            Births registered in England and Wales by age, sex, and local authority, with disclosure control applied, 2012 to 2020. Includes sex of child and age of mother at time of birth.

          • Death estimates

            Deaths registered in England and Wales by age, sex, and local authority, with disclosure control applied, 2012 to 2020. Includes age at death, sex coded to male and female and count within each group.

          • Energy Performance Certificates(EPC) for domestic buildings

            From Department for Levelling Up, Housing and Communities and the Office for National Statistics

            Open Data Energy Performance Certificates (EPCs) issued for domestic buildings constructed, sold or let since 2008. This data has been matched to the Office for National Statistics (ONS)s address index matching service.

          • GP registration estimates

            Number of individuals registered on the NHS Patient Register, aggregated by age, sex, year and local authority, 2016 to 2020.

          • International Passenger Survey estimates

            Origin-destination matrix by local authorities in the UK, by age, sex, and year, 2011 to March 2020. Includes expected counts of moves into and out of each local authority in England and Wales (including moves to and from Scotland and Northern Ireland) and in and out of the UK.

          • Mid-year population estimates

            Mid-year population estimates by age, sex and local authority, 20 June 2012 to 30 June 2020.

          • Prison estimates

            Number of prisoners held in establishments in England and Wales sentenced to six months or more as of 30 June each year. Aggregated counts for prisoners by prison, age, and sex, by local authority 2011 to 2020.

          • Statistical population dataset

            From Office for National Statistics

            Aggregated age and sex counts for population estimates by local authority and output area, 2011 to 2020. Sourced from Customer Information System, Higher Education Statistics Authority, English Schools Census and Welsh School Census, Personal Demographic Service, and Benefits and Income Database System

          • UK Regular Forces and Gurkhas estimates 2011 to 2020

            Aggregate age and sex counts for UK Regular Forces and Gurkhas, used in experimental modelling, 2011 to 2020.
